Northern Bobwhite quail are small birds that look like tiny chickens! They have:

  • Brown and white striped feathers that help them hide in grass
  • Strong legs for running fast on the ground
  • Short wings‚Äîthey only fly for short distances when they need to escape
  • Males have a white throat and eyebrow stripe

They're about the size of your hand and love to run instead of fly!

Northern Bobwhite quail are found in eastern Texas. You'll find them in:

  • Grasslands and fields üåæ
  • Brushy areas with lots of cover üåø
  • Farm edges and pastures

They like places where they can hide from predators and find lots of seeds!

üê£ Baby to Grown-Up

üß† Smart Survival Tricks (Adaptations)

Bobwhite quail are smart little birds! Here's how they stay safe:

  • Their brown feathers help them blend in with grass and leaves
  • They run zigzag to confuse predators
  • When danger comes, they burst into the air all at once‚Äîconfusing the predator!
  • They huddle together in a circle at night to stay warm
