üê£ Baby to Grown-Up

  • Female pheasants lay 10 to 12 eggs in a nest hidden in tall grass

üß† Smart Survival Tricks (Adaptations)

Ring-necked pheasants are smart and quick! Here's how they survive:

  • They can run up to 10 miles per hour to escape danger!
  • When surprised, they burst into the air with loud wing flaps to startle predators
  • Their long tails help them balance when running and flying
  • Females' brown feathers help them hide on their nests
